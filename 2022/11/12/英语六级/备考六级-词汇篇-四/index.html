新词速记

  2. urine n.尿
  3. recurrence n.再发生，循环，复发
  4. alongside adv.在旁边 ps:next to or at the side of of somebody
  5. choke v.噎住 n.窒息 ps：to make somebody unable to breath when drinkding some water or fluid
  6. ongoing adj.仍在进行的，不停的 ps:continuing to exist without stopping
  7. automation n.自动化 ps: the use of machine to do work that was done by people
  8. safeguard n.保卫措施
  9. redundancy n.多余，冗余 ps:redundant adj.多余的
  10. presumption n.推测，假设ps: the act of supposing that something is true
  11. contractor n.承包人 ps:contract v.承包，签订
  12. refutation n.反驳
  13. evadev.躲避 ps: to escape from somebody or avoid meeting somebody
  14. prone adj.俯卧的,有…的倾向
  15. notwithstanding prep.尽管，虽然
  16. ladder n.梯子ps:a device used for climbing that hao two long pieces of wood ,metal or rope with a series of steps
  17. predominate v.占…主要地位
  18. linger v.逗留
  19. trick n.恶作剧，诡计 v.哄骗 ps:play a trick on sb 捉弄某人

  25. dinev.用正餐，吃饭
  26. supervisorn.导师，监督员

  28. rehearse v.排练，预演ps:to practice or make people practice a play
  29. wary adj.谨慎的，小心的 ps:have a wary stare at somebody 谨慎地盯着某人
  30. ethnicadj.民族的ps: different ethnic groups 不同的民族
  31. preoccupy v.专注于，迷住ps:be preoccupied with worry about her son 满脑子都是担心她的儿子
  32. terrorist n.恐怖主义者
  33. reign v.占主导地位，统治 n.统治时期
  34. burial n.葬礼
  35. relish v.享受 n.调味品
